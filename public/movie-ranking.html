The Best Movies According To Ben

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Top Gun: Maverick

This is a really exciting, nail-biting movie for thrill seekers that are into fighter jets.

I Am Legend

A great sci-fi, zombie apocalipse movie to watch with the fam on a Friday night.

Twins

Twins is a funny family movie featuring Arnold Schwarzenegger, aka "the worlds strongest man" (back in his day)
